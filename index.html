Frequently Asked Questions

Is PictPath free to use?

Yes! PictPath is completely free to download and use. All core features including GPS tracking, photo capture, and trip sharing are free. Optional Premium ($0.99/month) removes ads.

Does it work without internet?

Absolutely! PictPath works fully offline. GPS tracking and photo capture work without internet connection. Your trips automatically sync when you're back online.

How do I share my trips?

After finishing a trip, tap the share button to generate a unique link. Anyone with the link can view your route and photos on an interactive map - no app needed!

How much battery does it use?

PictPath is optimized for battery efficiency. GPS tracking runs in the background with minimal impact. We recommend carrying a power bank for day-long adventures.

Is my data private?

Your trips are stored locally on your device. When you share a trip, only people with the link can see it. You're always in control of what you share.

When is the iOS version coming?

We're working hard on the iOS version! It's coming soon. Follow us for updates or check back regularly for the launch announcement.
