Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 23, 2025

Introduction

PictPath ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Location Data

PictPath collects GPS location data to track your route during trips. This data is:

  • Only collected when you actively start a trip
  • Stored locally on your device
  • Only uploaded to our servers if you choose to share a trip

Photos and Videos

Photos and videos you take within the app are:

  • Stored locally on your device
  • Tagged with GPS coordinates for map display
  • Only uploaded if you choose to share a trip

Account Information

If you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address (for authentication)
  • Display name (optional)

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Display your route and photos on the map
  • Enable trip sharing features
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve our app

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Your trip data is only shared when:

  • You explicitly choose to share a trip via a link
  • Required by law

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption. Trip data is stored on Supabase servers located in the EU.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export your trip data
  • Opt out of data collection by not using the app

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at:

contact@pictpath.com
